Shopping List:

  • Raspberry Pi 4 Kit

    Raspberry Pi 3 is also compatible.

  • Industrial Class 10 SD Card

    64GB recommended, but 32GB will also work.
  • Solid State Relay

    Two per heating zone, 40 amp or higher
  • Adafruit Universal Thermocouple Amplifier MAX31856

    One per temperature sensor
  • High Temperature K-Type Thermocouple

    One per temperature sensor
  • Red and Black Wire

    20 feet of each should be plenty
  • PiKiln Control Board

    This isn't currently available for sale.
  • Used Electric Kiln

    Check both Facebook Marketplace and Craigslist.

All of the products above have Amazon affiliate links, which if purchased a small percentage goes back to supporting this project. I would advise you to also check Ebay, AliExpress, or other online stores as you might find a better price elsewhere.

Buy The PiKiln Kit:

  • PiKiln Kit

    This includes all of the items above except for the kiln, some parts may be slightly different due to availability. PiKiln comes pre-installed on the Raspberry Pi and all of the sensors and relays have been tested and verified to be working. This isn't currently available for sale.
